getreceivedbyaddress

getreceivedbyaddress "address" ( minconf )

Returns the total amount received by the given address in transactions with at least minconf confirmations.

Argument #1 - address

Type: string, required

The bitcoin address for transactions.

Argument #2 - minconf

Type: numeric, optional, default=1

Only include transactions confirmed at least this many times.

Result

Name Type Description
n numeric The total amount in DGB received at this address.

Examples

The amount from transactions with at least 1 confirmation:

digiasset_core-cli getreceivedbyaddress "D7hrf5D21PV24ksEGRdYCZ6JqgsboSVKKC

The amount including unconfirmed transactions, zero confirmations:

digiasset_core-cli getreceivedbyaddress "D7hrf5D21PV24ksEGRdYCZ6JqgsboSVKKC 0

The amount with at least 6 confirmations:

digiasset_core-cli getreceivedbyaddress "D7hrf5D21PV24ksEGRdYCZ6JqgsboSVKKC 6

As a JSON-RPC call:

curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id": "curltest", "method": "getreceivedbyaddress", "params": ["D7hrf5D21PV24ksEGRdYCZ6JqgsboSVKKC 6]}' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:14022/
